Reader guidance

Content Warning

This site discusses historical racism, antisemitism, ableism, coercive sterilization, institutionalization, and state violence.

What Readers May Encounter

  • Descriptions of forced sterilization and coercive reproductive policy.
  • Discussion of Nazi racial hygiene and the murder of disabled people.
  • Historical terms and claims that are racist, ableist, antisemitic, or dehumanizing.
  • Primary-source material that may be upsetting when the archive is expanded.

How Materials Are Framed

Harmful historical claims are included only to explain and critique them. The project rejects the ideas being documented and avoids reproducing slurs or propaganda imagery unless there is a clear educational reason and adequate context.
