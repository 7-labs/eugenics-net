Mission and scope

About the Project

The site is designed as a serious educational and archival resource, not a keyword traffic project.

Mission

The Eugenics History & Bioethics Archive documents, contextualizes, and critiques the history of eugenics, scientific racism, forced sterilization, genetic discrimination, and their modern legacies.

What This Project Does

  • Explains eugenics as pseudoscience and policy history.
  • Connects historical harms to modern bioethics and genomics debates.
  • Provides teaching guidance for responsible classroom use.
  • Builds an archive model where primary sources are contextualized before publication.

What This Project Does Not Do

  • It does not advocate eugenics or genetic hierarchy.
  • It does not provide reproductive, medical, or genetic counseling advice.
  • It does not publish unreviewed user submissions or open forums.
  • It does not use the domain for affiliate content, ads, or sensational traffic capture.

Current Status

This is a first foundation release in pre-launch hardening. The public corrections route is corrections@eugenics.net, and the project still needs source review, old-URL audit decisions, and expert or community review before publishing sensitive archive materials.
