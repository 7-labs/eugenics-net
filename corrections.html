Corrections and Contact

The project welcomes corrections about factual accuracy, source quality, historical context, accessibility, and wording that could unintentionally reproduce harm.

Send a Correction

Email corrections@eugenics.net with the page URL, the issue, and any stable source that supports the correction. For source concerns, include the citation or archive reference when available.

What We Review

  • Factual errors, outdated wording, broken citations, and missing context.
  • Content warnings, accessibility issues, and language that needs clearer anti-endorsement framing.
  • Archive-source concerns, provenance issues, or requests to add affected-community context.

Boundaries

This address is not a medical, reproductive, genetic counseling, legal, or crisis-support service. It is also not a route for advocacy of eugenics, racial hierarchy, disability devaluation, antisemitism, forced sterilization, or genetic discrimination.
